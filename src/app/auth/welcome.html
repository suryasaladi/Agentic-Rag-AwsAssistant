Cloud Ops Assistant

Chat with your live AWS account and your team's runbooks — ask what's running, check health, look up procedures, and safely start or stop resources.

  • See your resources — "What EC2 instances do I have?", "List my S3 buckets."
  • Diagnose health — "Is anything unhealthy?" — it checks live status and alarms.
  • Search runbooks — "What's the rollback procedure for a failed deploy?"
  • Take actions — start / stop / reboot EC2, always with a confirmation step.

Why an AWS Access Key & Secret?

To read your resources and act on your behalf, the assistant needs AWS credentials for your account. They're used only for your session, kept in the server's memory, and never written to disk. Use a least-privilege or short-lived key, and sign out any time.
